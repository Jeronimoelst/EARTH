What is Earth: Water, trash n food?

Is an organization that is looking to give a new vision of our world and how our fingerprint is affecting us, looking ways to solve worldwide basic issues that on some places are fixed and some others do not; using the internet of value to spread rewards and provide value to the people actions.

Water:

Our main resource for life on EARTH that is being wasted without control and needs to be properly distributed between human beings.

Trash:

Non natural resources that are being made by us to fulfill some needs, but at the end of the day turns into non useful items occupying space on our limited EARTH.

Food:

Just like the Water, a main resource that is being wasted by someones while some others have none, a controlled production and distribution needs to be implemented to meet the goals.
